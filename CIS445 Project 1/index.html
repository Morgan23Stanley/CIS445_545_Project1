Brainiac

Brainiac

Social Education
Website

At the start of September, I had a vision of a better education system. The problem with learning any subject is having to rely solely on the teacher's knowledge and style of teaching. Many of us have a unique way of studying, memorizing, and retaining information. Some of us learn better through visuals, while others learn through audio or written words. Brainiac is an all in one app, that allows students/learners to learn through their preferred method. It will take course books and intructors to post videos on the specific subject. Along with videos, there will be audio and plain text. There are so many other features that Brainiac offers. I plan to release the product after I graduate college.

Power Bi

Power Bi

Social Education
Website

During the summer of 2023, I had the privelege of interning at Illinois Mutual as a Data Analyst. I worked with company dataset to create dashboards and reports. These dashboard were made by taking corporation's data and using visuals to explain the results. For example, given a dataset of agents with demographics and where they sold products throughout the country, I used heat maps to show which area of the country most products are sold. Other visuals used were pie charts to show the amount of certain products were sold, bar graphs to show the number of male vs female, and cards that display aggregated data. I also learned a lot about data relationships, using SQL, and using DAX.
